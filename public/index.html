Florida Man steals $360 worth of wine from grocery store

KEY LARGO, Fla. (WFLA) - A woman was arrested on a warrant in the Florida Keys for stealing numerous bottles of alcohol from a store. A Monroe County sheriff's deputy was called to a Winn Dixie store on March 23, where managers of the store told him a woman, later identified as Melissa Link, 39, was caught attempting to shoplift a bottle of sake from the store in her purse. One of the managers told the deputy the issue was continuous with the woman. The manager identified her by a previous mugshot. The manager advised the deputy that Link had stolen two bottles of sake, as well as numerous bottles of wine over a five month period, valued at over $750. In April, store management verified there was a total of 60 incidents dating from Nov. 14, 2017 to March 23 where 58 bottles of Pinot Grigio and 2 bottles of sake were taken, for a total loss of $756. A warrant was issued for Link's arrest for 60 counts of retail theft. Link was found by a deputy in Key Largo on June 20. Link was continuously aggressive towards deputies and at one point, was hit with pepper spray.
